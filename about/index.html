about


This site is built with technology from the amazing Middleman static site generator. It uses the beauty of Ruby language and the utility of the Padrino framework behind the scenes to process and built a complete site.


Zurb Foundation is the structure behind the layout and responsive features. Try resizing your browser window or rotating your viewing screen - the layout responds to use the space.


And lastly, you might notice a vintage computer display as a motif throughout my site. It's actually a Digital Equipment Corporation VT05 terminal. This groundbreaking corporation sold this sleek interface to their mainframe computers in the early 70's. The DEC company is gone but their innovations have found their way into most computing devices that surround us. Respect.
